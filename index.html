About Me

A Little About Me...

My name is Kim. I'm a Mountain View, California girl living the Colorado lifestyle in beautiful Fort Collins. A recent Front End Bootcamp grad, I am making a career transition to web design and couldn't be more eager to keep advancing my skills. My first experience with web design was back in the day on MySpace, styling my profile every few weeks. If I knew then that was something that could have led down this career path sooner I wouldn't have believed you.

I'm passionate about the small things. The tiny details that make something more elegant or clean. The invisible designs that wow you and enhance your experience rather than distract and detract. My strengths are UX/UI web design, mockup production, presentations and copy writing/content creation. My goal is to find an internship with a great team, that is always pushing for the best. I learn through experience, and reptetition. Throw me in the deep end with the team and I will figure it out.

When not working, you will find me in the mountains, on the bike path, in my workshop or binging a good show along side my pug Yoshi and my partner, Jeff.
